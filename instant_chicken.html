Instant Chicken Masala

Surprise your guests by spending time with them yet, serve them this 'delicious' preparation.

Instant chicken Masala

Description

Maisa Instant Chicken Masala is made from selected spices and is prepared under most hygienic conditions. Maisa Instant Chicken Masala makes very dish tastes special with its rich aroma and flavour.It is a perfect blend of spices so as you can use it in all types of Non-veg curries.

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon
  • Cashew
  • Cloves
  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Dry Ginger
  • Coconut Powder
  • Fenugreek and Refined Oil (Powder Form)
  • Garlic Powder
  • Mace
  • Onion Powder
  • Poppy Seeds
  • Red Chillies
  • Star Anise
  • Salt
  • Turmeric

How to Prepare

For Fry: Take 500gm of chicken into your desired pieces. Add 300ml water and boil for 10min. Then mix with Maisa Instant Chicken Masala . Cook for 10min. Your desired dish ready to serve.
For Curry: Take 500gm of chicken into your desired pieces. Add 600ml water and boil for 10min. Then mix with Maisa Instant Chicken Masala . Cook for 10min. Your desired dish ready to serve.
Note : Add little salt or chilli powder as your taste.
