Chicken Masala

Description

Maisa Chicken Masala is made from selected spices and is prepared under most hygienic conditions. Maisa Chicken Masala makes very dish tastes special with its rich aroma and flavour. It can be used for other Veg./Non-veg. Recipes also.

Ingredients

  • Black Pepper
  • Cinnamon
  • Coriander Seeds
  • Cloves
  • Cumin
  • Lodizedi Salt
  • Red Chillies
  • Star Anise
  • Turmeric

How to Prepare

Take 500gm of finely chopped chicken pieces into a clean bowl. Add 1tsp. Maisa Ginger Garlic past to chicken pieces, marinate for 30 minutes. Fry 2 chopped onions, 4 chopped green chillies, 1tsp. Maisa Ginger Garlic Past in 2tbsp. of Oil. Add marinated chicken mix along with 2 chopped Tomatos. Add 2tsp. and cook for 10-15 minutes.
Note: It can be used for other Veg./Non-veg. Recipes also.
